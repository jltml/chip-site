CHIP aims to bring health care for your child into Michiana homes in 2025.

Complete Infusion Care

Children Home Infusion Pharmacy (CHIP) knows there is no comfort quite like being home with family. That's why our total home care model is designed to meet your infusion needs. Our services include anti-infectives, pain control, parenteral nutrition, custom compounding, and chemotherapy.

Inspiration

Michael Fisher's (co-founder) nephew has kidney necrosis. Despite his disability, TJ is unstoppable. He learned to walk before learning to crawl. It's a shame that he spends hours every week in the car traveling to receive infusions. CHIP offers the same hospital quality care in the comfort of home. We deliver your child's medication and compact equipment directly to you. CHIP provides a local and convenient choice for your infusion needs so that children with medical complexities, like TJ, can be happy at home with family.
